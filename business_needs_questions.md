# Business needs questions that drive architectural value

## Problem and purpose

- What problem does this software solve, and for whom?
- What happens in this problem goes unsolved? (reveals true priority)

## Scale & context

- Whats's the realistic scale now and in 2-3 years? (avoid over engineering)
- What's the organization context (startup, enterprise, regulated industry)?

## Contraints

- What are hard constraints?
  - budget, team size, deadlines, compliance/regulation
- What are soft constraints?
  - existing tech stack, team skills, integrations

## Change velocity

- Which parts of the business change frequently vs. stay stable?
  - defines where architectural flexibility is worth investing
- Who owns the requirements?
  - one stakeholder or many competing ones?

## Sucess criteria

- How does the business measure success for this software?
- What would "good enough" look like at each phase?

## Risk tolerance

- What failure modes are acceptable, and which are catastrophic?
